# User Personas

## Persona 1: The Calorie Tracker

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Name** | Alex |
| **Role** | Health-conscious individual focused on nutrition tracking |
| **Age Range** | 25-45 |
| **Tech Savviness** | Moderate to high |
| **Primary Goal** | Track daily calorie and nutrient intake to meet health goals |
| **Motivation** | Lose weight, maintain fitness, or manage dietary conditions |
| **Pain Points** | Manual calorie counting is tedious; estimating portions is inaccurate; forgetting to log meals |
| **Key Behaviors** | Logs meals 2-4 times daily, checks progress charts weekly, adjusts goals monthly |

### Needs
- Quick meal logging (photo snap or fast manual entry)
- Accurate calorie and nutrition estimates
- Visual progress toward daily/weekly goals
- Historical data to identify patterns
- Offline access for logging when without internet

### Frustrations
- Inaccurate portion estimates from generic food databases
- Too many taps to log a simple meal
- Losing data when switching devices
- No feedback on whether daily intake is balanced

## Persona 2: The Home Cook

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Name** | Sam |
| **Role** | Home cook who wants to make healthy meals with available ingredients |
| **Age Range** | 28-50 |
| **Tech Savviness** | Moderate |
| **Primary Goal** | Get recipe suggestions that use available ingredients and fit nutrition goals |
| **Motivation** | Reduce food waste, eat healthier, save time on meal decisions |
| **Pain Points** | Not knowing what to cook with what's in the fridge; recipes that require too many extra ingredients; meals that don't fit dietary goals |
| **Key Behaviors** | Checks fridge 1-2 times per week, browses recipes before cooking, prefers quick meals on weekdays |

### Needs
- Easy way to capture what's in the fridge (photo or list)
- Recipe suggestions that match available ingredients
- Recipes filtered by cooking time and skill level
- Nutrition info for suggested recipes
- Dietary preference support (vegetarian, keto, etc.)

### Frustrations
- Recipes requiring ingredients not on hand
- No consideration of nutrition goals in recipe apps
- Complex recipes when short on time
- Having to manually cross-reference ingredients with what's available
